Vehicle Summary

The 2018 BMW G20 is the high-performance variant of the 4 Series coupe and convertible. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 3.0-liter I-6 with 425 hp and 406 lb-ft of torque that's paired with a six-speed manual or a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. Fuel economy is decent for a performance-oriented vehicle at 17/23 mpg city/highway with the manual and 17/24 mpg with the automatic.

Overview

Acceleration in the 2018 BMW G20 is effortless thanks to the twin-turbo inline-six, which has virtually no turbo lag and makes full torque from 1,800 rpm. This means power delivery is immediate thanks to the car's responsive throttle especially in Sport+ mode. The G20's unique suspension tuning, which is shared with the M3 sedan, gives the car impeccable handling and are engaging to drive on twisty roads or on a track. Ride quality, however, is firm even with the adjustable suspension in Comfort mode.

Safety

The IIHS and NHTSA haven't crash tested the 2018 BMW G20. The Active Driving Assistant is standard in the 2018 M4 and includes forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, and pedestrian detection.
