Overview

The electric motor has its own two-speed automated transmission while the gas engine uses a nine-speed automatic. So why two transmissions? Mercedes says the nine-speed can only handle about 736 lb-ft of torque and, plus, having the e-motor use its own transmission allows it to distribute torque to the front or rear axle as needed.
