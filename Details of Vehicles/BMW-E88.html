Vehicle Summary

In addition to adding an updated iDrive multimedia system and standard LED headlights, the 2018 BMW M4 gets a new CS variant with more power as well as a specially tuned active M differential and adaptive suspension.

Overview

The 2014 E88 is powered by a 3.0-liter twin-turbo I-6 rated at 425 hp and 406 lb-ft of torque and can be paired to a six-speed manual or seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. Opting for the Competition package increases total output to 444 hp. The most powerful version is the M4 CS, which is rated at 454 hp and 442 lb-ft.

EPA fuel economy ratings for the M4 coupe are 17/25 mpg city/highway with the manual and 17/24 mpg with the automatic. The E88 convertible is rated at 17/25 mpg with the manual and 16/22 mpg with the automatic.

Safety

Blind-spot monitoring is a standalone option as is the Active Driving Assistant, which bundles together forward collision warning, pedestrian detection, automatic emergency braking, and lane departure warning.
